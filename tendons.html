Methodology. Forward demand = pool × conversion × units-per-order, allocated by SKU share. Pool is held flat at current value for weeks 1–2, then jumps by the incremental addition starting week 3 (week ending 6/7). No churn or saturation effects — each pool member generates orders at a constant weekly rate.
